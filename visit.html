Visit us

Our museum is a place where you can explore, learn, and discover new ideas. Whether you enjoy history, science, art, or simply finding out how things came to be, there is something here for you.

Location

The museum is located in Kongens gate 1, 7011 Trondheim. There is free parking up to 7 hours for guests and visitors.

Admission

The entrance is free. Guided tour: 70 NOK per person. If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us by mail to arrange the tour. Email: CSMTrondheim@gmail.no

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
